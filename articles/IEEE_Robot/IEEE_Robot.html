IEEE Robot

2014 I lead my school's IEEE group in creating a robot to compete in a yearly competition in Kentucky. Sadly I didn't document much of it at the time but I do have some remnants one aspect.

This circuit board will hold four pololu stepper motor drivers that will be controlling the four mecanum wheels. The micro stepping setting for the controllers is changeable via the solder gaps seen going down the center of the board.
