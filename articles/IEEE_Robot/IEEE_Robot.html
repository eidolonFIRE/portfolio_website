IEEE Robot

I have just completed a circuit board for our VCU-IEEE robot. It will hold four pololu stepper motor drivers that will be controlling the four mecanum wheels.

This spring break my team will be driving down to Kentucky to compete in the IEEE hardware competition and we need to have a reliable setup! This board will be able to handle the amount of power we need as well as be re-configurable. Notice the solder gabs at the center of each driver mounting. Those pads are accessible via the bottom and when the gab is soldered together it changes the micro-stepping mode of the driver. This should be set long before the competition but in the event it needs changed last minute... just grab an iron!
