Magical Keepsake Box

Summary:

For Christmas of 2015 I decided to make my girlfriend a special hand-crafted gift. I wanted to make something mechanical and elegant yet functional and within the limits of what I could fabricate on the carpet floor of my apartment. While scrounging my brain for something sufficient, I conjured up this magical box.

I made a wooden keepsake box with a magnetic lock. The lock has a complexity of 2^16 and requires BOTH of the wooden medallions to open (this is symbolic). To open, simply place the corresponding medallions on each picture and rotate slightly in an outward direction. The sound of the bolt sliding through the lock is very satisfying.

Materials:

  • Sides
    • Mahogany (home depot)
    • 5.5" wide boards
  • Top, Bottom, Lock
    • Red Oak (home depot)
    • 11.25" x 18.2" (very close to golden ratio)
  • Stain (same for all)
    • Minwax Red Mahogany 225 (home depot)
  • Felt liner
  • Hinges
  • Magnets
    • Internal lock x 16 D38
    • Medallions x 16 D68-N52
