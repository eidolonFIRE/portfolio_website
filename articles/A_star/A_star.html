A* Implementation

At some point everyone makes their own implementation of the A* algorithm, and this one's mine. I enjoyed trying new heiristics and comparing how they perform. There are 5 different heiristics you can try in the GUI. "Directional" is my favorite.

The gui shows you each heiristic's cost and resulting path length. They each have their advantages and preform better in particular cercumstances.

Try the gui out for yourself. Source available on A_star GitHub.
